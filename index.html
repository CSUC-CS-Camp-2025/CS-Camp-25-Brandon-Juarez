Yellow-Banded Poison Dart Frog

Geographic Location:

Facts about this Frog:

Nourthern South America

They Yellow Banded Poison Dart frog is mostly active during the day. One of the largest poison dart frogs being about 2 inches. They eat insects like ants, spiders, termites, and crickets. Each frog has a unique pattern so you wont find any other frog that look alike.
